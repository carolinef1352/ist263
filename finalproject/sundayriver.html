Sunday River

Sunday River, a large mountain in Maine, provides a family-oriented atmosphere to its customers. There are many options for lodging and there are 8 peaks to ski, so you will never see it all!

Level of Difficulty: Beginner, Intermediate, & Advanced

Social Scene: Family & College

Conditions: Impeccable

Food: 10/10

Restaurant Recommendations: Foggy Goggle, Shipyard, Matterhorn, Sliders
