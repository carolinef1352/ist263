Greek Peak

This mountain in Upstate New York has proven to be a great way to escape the winters while at Syracuse. Although it is a smaller mountain, it is a great place to night ski with your friends.

Level of Difficulty: Beginner

Social Scene: Family and Kids

Conditions: Decent (sometimes iced off)

Food:10/10

Restaurant Recommendations: Trax, Greek Peak Mountain Resort
