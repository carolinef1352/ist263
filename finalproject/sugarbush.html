Sugarbush

Although it’s definitely cold here, the conditions were hard to beat. For those northeastern skiers looking to go out west, this is definitely the closest you’ll get.

Level of Difficulty: Advanced

Social Scene: College (skiing wise) and Adult (Town/area wise)

Conditions: Impeccable (with the exception of moguls)

Food 5/10 (not many options)

Restaurant Recommendations: Fit to be Thai’d
